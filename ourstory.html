Renu Bali - Empowering Through Education and Enterprise




Ms. Renu Bali, founder and director of Tender Heart NGO, left her position as a lecturer at Delhi University to uplift underprivileged and distinctively abled individuals through education. Inspired by her own educational opportunities, she started Tender Heart with two students, the children of a local milkman. Despite resistance from villagers, Ms. Bali persevered, establishing a comprehensive school and employment opportunities for rural women, making them financially independent. To support their mission, Tender Heart launched the brand 'Zoonie', selling products made by rural women and special needs individuals, embodying the NGO's commitment to self-sustainability and communityÂ empowerment.
